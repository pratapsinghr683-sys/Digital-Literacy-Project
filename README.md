## Module Summaries

### Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic (Module 1)
Designed a one-page visual infographic using Canva that covers the definition of digital literacy, useful digital tools for students, safe internet practices, professional online presence, and email etiquette. The infographic was designed to be beginner-friendly and shareable with peers.

### Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio (Module 2)
Set up and updated profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. Created a GitHub Profile README with personal details, branch, year, and learning goals. Filled in the Education section on LinkedIn and set up a beginner Kaggle profile.

### Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms (Module 3)
Created an account on HackerRank and completed the "Solve Me First" beginner challenge. Also built a 5-question Google Form titled *"Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz"* for batch use, complete with multiple choice and short answer questions.

**Google Form Link (Digital Literacy Quiz):**
> https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSey1IR1pwTJOLdqR91cbMUjioNkIapKc9nQZRbpQmpPmRHdlQ/viewform?usp=publish-editor

### Task 4 – Professional Email & Etiquette Guide (Module 4)
Drafted two professional emails: one to a professor requesting a deadline extension, and one to an internship coordinator expressing interest in a summer internship. Also created a Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist with 5 do's and 5 don'ts for college students.

### Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness Case Study (Module 5)
Researched UPI/Online Payment Fraud as the chosen cybercrime type. Wrote a 200–250 word case study covering what the crime is, how it happens step-by-step, who is targeted, and consequences. Produced a "Stay Safe Online" checklist with 8+ tips including 2 UPI-specific tips and reporting channels (cybercrime.gov.in, Helpline 1930).
